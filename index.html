The Research‑Backed Funnel Playbook

  1. Choose a single, winnable promise. “Get [Result] in [Time] without [Common Pain].”
  2. Pick the right starting template. Lead Gen + App; Product Sales + Bump + 2 Upsells; or Webinar/VSL + Membership.
  3. Draft copy like an adult. Benefit headline → specific subhead → outcome bullets → proof → risk reversal.
  4. Wire the money pages. Add a 60‑second bump and a single, relevant one‑click upsell.
  5. Follow up like a pro. 5‑email workflow: Welcome → Value → Proof → FAQ/Objections → Deadline; branch by behavior.
